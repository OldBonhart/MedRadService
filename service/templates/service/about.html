About Med Rad Industries

For prediction, we use a neural network with the ResNet architecture, trained on the X-Ray Dataset of the National Institute of Health Chest

This NIH Chest X-ray Dataset is comprised of 112,120 X-ray images with disease labels from 30,805 unique patients. To create these labels, the authors used Natural Language Processing to text-mine disease classifications from the associated radiological reports.

We also created a convenient panel for visualizing explanations from our neural network using gradient localization [Grad-CAM], so that the radiologist can examine the prediction in detail.
